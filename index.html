Julie Balboni

Hi, I’m a data analyst with a background in technical support and quality assurance. It was in these roles where I discovered my love of working with data - understanding relationships, interactions, and patterns – and helping both internal and external customers understand theirs. I care deeply about helping others seek information from data and developing insights. The delight of a customer seeing accurate, intuitive visualizations is unmistakable; transforming data into actionable insights positively impacts both a person’s day and business value.

I gained experience with data analytics through an intensive training program and project work which allowed me to enhance previous skills while developing new ones. My portfolio below shows a sampling of my work ranging from visualizations created in Excel and Tableau to Python code in Jupyter Notebook and SQL queries in PostGreSQL as well as some of the trends and insights identified.

As a lifelong learner, you will often find me reading and developing new projects. I cherish time with my tech crazy family, love to explore the world through travel, relish outdoor time during the warmer months in Wisconsin, and run and play tennis.
